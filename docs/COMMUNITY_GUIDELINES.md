## üìú Code of Conduct

### ‚úÖ Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with kindness and professionalism
- **Be Constructive**: Offer feedback that helps others improve
- **Be Patient**: Remember everyone is learning at their own pace
- **Be Open-Minded**: Welcome diverse perspectives and approaches
- **Be Helpful**: Share knowledge and support community members
- **Be Authentic**: Use your real identity or consistent pseudonym
- **Be Solution-Oriented**: Focus on fixing problems, not assigning blame

### üö´ Prohibited Activities

#### Tier 1 - Immediate Ban (Zero Tolerance)

- **Harassment & Hate Speech**: Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or identity
- **Doxxing**: Sharing personal information without consent
- **Scams & Fraud**: Impersonation, phishing, or financial manipulation
- **Illegal Content**: Sharing content that violates laws or regulations
- **Sexual Content**: NSFW material or unwanted advances
- **Threats**: Violence, intimidation, or blackmail

#### Tier 2 - Warning ‚Üí Temporary Ban ‚Üí Permanent Ban

- **Spam**: Excessive self-promotion, duplicate posts, or bot-like behavior
- **Misinformation**: Deliberately spreading false technical or financial information
- **Market Manipulation**: Coordinated pump/dump schemes or fake hype
- **Trolling**: Intentionally disruptive or inflammatory behavior
- **Plagiarism**: Claiming others' work as your own
- **Ban Evasion**: Creating alt accounts to bypass moderation

#### Tier 3 - Warning ‚Üí Timeout

- **Off-Topic Spam**: Repeatedly posting in wrong channels
- **Low-Effort Content**: "Wen token?" "Wen moon?" without context
- **Excessive Tagging**: Pinging moderators/team members unnecessarily
- **Cross-Posting**: Spamming same message across multiple channels

## üõ†Ô∏è Dispute Resolution Process

### Step 1: Self-Resolution (24 hours)
If you have a conflict with another member:
1. Take a break before responding emotionally
2. Try to resolve privately via DM if appropriate
3. Assume good intent - misunderstandings happen

### Step 2: Community Moderator (48 hours)
If self-resolution fails:
1. Contact a moderator via DM with:
   - Summary of the issue
   - Relevant screenshots/links
   - What resolution you're seeking
2. Moderator will review and mediate if necessary
3. Both parties will be heard fairly

### Step 3: Core Team Escalation (72 hours)
For serious or unresolved issues:
1. Case escalates to core team (Pincer/Herald)
2. Full investigation with evidence review
3. Final decision is binding

### Step 4: Appeals (7 days)
For bans or major penalties:
1. Submit appeal via official form
2. Include new evidence or context
3. One appeal per incident allowed
4. Decision reviewed by different team member

## üì£ Reporting Violations

### How to Report
1. **In Discord**: Use `/report` command or DM moderators
2. **Urgent Issues**: Tag `@Moderators` in incident channel
3. **Anonymous**: Fill out [Report Form] (private & confidential)
4. **Sensitive/Legal**: Email conduct@pincerprotocol.io

### What Happens Next
- **Response Time**: < 24 hours for acknowledgment
- **Investigation**: Evidence reviewed by 2+ moderators
- **Action**: Warning, timeout, or ban based on severity
- **Notification**: Reporter informed of outcome (privacy permitting)
- **Transparency**: Serious cases summarized publicly (details anonymized)
